Photography Introduction:

Binary Life

Binary Life (2015)—a series of six black-and-white silhouette portraits—depict darkened profiles of cell phone-holding subjects gesturing a range of emotions. I wanted to portray a detached and lonely reality of human reliance on technology and stress an alienation and lack of contact between subjects in separate photos facing back to back.
