Tom's Favorite Singers and Writers





One ought, every day, to hear a song, read a fine poem, and, if possible, to speak a few reasonable words.

--Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

The two most important things that accompany me in my life are music and books. I listen to music almost whenever I can. Genres or languages are not limitations to what I love to listen. From Chinese to English, from pop to country, I enjoyed fantastic rhythms and melodies from various artists.
Books are the one and only what keeps me addicted in holidays. Beginning in college, I've been keeping the habit of reading books in my summer and winter vacations. As long as it's a famous piece of work, I'm always willing to experience it.
On this website, I want to present to you my two favorite singers and two favorite novelists with different nationalities:

  • Taylor Swift
  • A-Lin
  • Louis Cha
  • Haruki Murakami
