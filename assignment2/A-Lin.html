A-Lin(黄丽玲)







A-Lin (Chinese: 阿玲; pinyin: Ā Líng), also known by her birth name Huang Li Ling (traditional Chinese: 黃麗玲; simplified Chinese: 黄丽玲; pinyin: Huáng Lìlíng), Amis name Lisang Pacidal Koyouan, is an aboriginal Taiwanese pop singer, lyricist, and occasional composer of Amis descent.

While A-Lin considers herself as an Contralto, she has been noted to have an extremely wide vocal range, as noted in many of her songs to have over two octaves or more.
